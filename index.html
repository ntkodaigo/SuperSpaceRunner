SuperSpaceRunner

Description.

The game that I created in the final assignment of the course Game Development for Modern Platforms by Michigan State University in Coursera. Super Space Runner is a Action Platform Endless Runner game in 2D. You are in control of a spaceship, which has to travel through space, collecting coins, power bananas. and some special supplements, in the way you expect a meteor shower and some alien enemies will not let you go very far!

Instructions

Collect coins, bananas, powerups, skills and avoid meteors and enemies, and REACH HIGH! Controls: Keyboard = WASD or arrow keys to move between grids XBox360 Controller and PSX Controller Compatible.
